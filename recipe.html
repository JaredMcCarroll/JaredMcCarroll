Scrambled Eggs

Ingridients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Cream
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
    • ___________________________________________________________________________________________

    Instructions

    1. Cook butter in pan and let butter melt
    2. Crack the eggs and put them in the pan to cook
    3. Whisk the eggs and mix them so they become scrambled, then add the Cream
    4. whenever you feel like the eggs are done take them out and put them on a plate
    5. Add salt and pepper to taste
    6. Enjoy!
